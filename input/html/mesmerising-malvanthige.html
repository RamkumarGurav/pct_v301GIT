About Malvanthige Adventure Tour: A Thrilling Off-Beat Experience Embark on a two-day escapade with Malvanthige Adventure Tour, designed to transport you to a realm of absolute thrills and off-beat wonders. This Malvanthige Adventure Tour promises not just excitement but a tapestry of memories etched in the landscapes of TB Beach, the thrill of water parks, the serenity of tea estates, and the breathtaking vistas of Elneer Falls and Kyathanmakki View Point.

Things to Carry

  • Small backpack to carry your essentials during the trek
  • Power Bank
  • Water bottles - 2 (1 liter each)
  • Lunch Box & Spoon to carry your packed lunch
  • A comfortable pair of shoes with good grip for trekking
  • Flip-flops for lazing around the campsite
  • Torch/Headlamp to be used at night Portable chargers for your electronics
  • Extra pair of clothes to change into once wet
  • Polybags to pack your wet clothes
  • Extra cash for meals not included in the itinerary Carry at least one Id proof (Driving License, Voter Id, or Aadhar card)

Tour Plan

  • 7:00 PM : Depart from Bangalore for TB Beach.
  • Enjoy a road trip filled with lively music and cool breeze.
  • 6:00 AM : Arrive at TB Beach. Coffee or tea with tasty bites while enjoying the sunrise.
  • 8:00 AM : Freshen up and have a hearty breakfast.
  • 9:00 AM : Head to the Amusement Park for a day of water activities and fun.
  • 1:00 PM : Lunch at the amusement park.
  • 2:30 PM : Depart for Malvanthige Estate, a 3-hour journey through scenic ghat roads. Enjoy old songs and stunning views of the Western Ghats.
  • 6:00 PM : Arrival at the campsite. Welcome with a refreshing drink and time to refresh.
  • 7:00 PM : B.B.Q. session with a choice of marinations. Bonfire, music, dance, and unlimited fun. Local cuisine dinner under the stars
  • 10:00 PM : Sign off for the day in the cozy tents.
  • Early Morning : Wake up to the dawn, freshen up, and energize with a coffee shot.
  • 7:00 AM : Embark on a tea estate walk to absorb the fantastic views of the tea estate mountains.
  • 9:00 AM : Return for breakfast and prepare for a thrilling jeep ride towards Elneer Falls.
  • 12:00 PM : Enjoy the refreshing waterfalls and proceed to an off-road ride to Kyathanmakki View Point.
  • Afternoon : Have lunch on the way, capturing the essence of the journey.
  • Evening : Head back to Bangalore, concluding an unforgettable adventure.
